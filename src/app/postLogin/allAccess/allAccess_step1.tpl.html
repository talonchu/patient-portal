Make Intellivisit Yours

Customize your experience by choosing a provider network.

Intellivisit partners with local teams of nurses and doctors to bring you the best care possible. Each time you submit an Intellivisit, a nurse or doctor from one of these excellent teams will review your information and create a plan to help you feel better.

On the next screen, you will see one or more teams that are eligible to serve you based on who you are and where you live. You will be asked to select which organization you would like to review your Intellivisits. You'll only have to do this once, and you can always change your mind later.
